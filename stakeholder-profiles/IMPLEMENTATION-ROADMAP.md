## Overview: From Pilot to Scale

```
PHASE 1: PILOT (Days 1-30)
  → 5 executives (CEO, CFO, SVP Ops, VP Sales, Controller)
  → Proof of concept + measurement
  → Validate business case + secure scaling investment

PHASE 2: EXPANSION (Days 31-60)
  → 15 leadership team members (add 10 director/manager level)
  → Replicate successful patterns across functions
  → Build organizational capability

PHASE 3: SCALE (Days 61-90)
  → 50+ individual contributors
  → Democratize access across organization
  → Establish sustainable operating model
```

3. **Quality**: Executive satisfaction 7.5+/10, team satisfaction 7+/10
