## DESIGN TIPS

**Colors:**
- Primary: Professional Blue (#0066CC)
- Success: Growth Green (#00AA66)
- Urgency: Action Orange (#FF6600)
- Background: White or Light Gray (#F5F5F5)

**Icons to Search in Canva:**
- "Line icons" (clean, professional)
- "Business icons"
- "Technology icons"
- "Process icons"

**Keep It Simple:**
- One main visual per slide
- White space is your friend
- Consistent style throughout
- Bold key numbers (ROI, time savings)
