# Sorensen Value Metrics: Complete ROI Calculation

**Executive Summary:** Claude transforms Sorensen's revenue operations from reactive to predictive, generating $1.2M-2M in incremental annual revenue while recapturing 60-70% of operational time. This document quantifies every benefit with underlying assumptions and sensitivity analysis.

---

## I. Revenue Impact Framework

### Strategic Focus Areas
1. **Sales Pipeline Acceleration** - Faster close cycles, deal recovery
2. **Forecast Accuracy** - Reliable forecasting = better board decisions
3. **Customer Retention** - Proactive churn prevention
4. **Expansion Revenue** - Systematic identification of upsell/cross-sell
5. **Marketing Efficiency** - Faster campaign velocity = more pipeline

### Revenue Impact Calculation Methodology
- **Revenue Impact** = (Incremental Pipeline) × (Win Rate) × (Probability)
- **Time Savings** = (Hours Saved) × (Cost Per Hour)
- **Efficiency Gains** = Additional customers/revenue serviced with same headcount

**Problem Solved**: ±18% forecast variance creates $90K monthly swing risk (18% of $5M)

**Improvement Mechanism**:
- Claude applies rep-specific probability weighting based on historical close rates
- Claude identifies stalling deals through velocity analysis (days in stage vs. peer average)
- Claude flags activity misalignment (high-stage deals with low recent activity)
- Result: Probability-weighted forecast vs. face-value forecast

**Quantification**:

|--------|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| Forecast variance | ±18% | ±8% | 55% tighter |
| Monthly forecast range | $4.1M - $5.9M | $4.6M - $5.4M | $800K narrower range |
| Forecast-to-actual variance | 15-20% of target | 5-10% of target | 2-3x more accurate |

**Business Value**:
- **Board Decision Quality**: Board can plan resources with 55% higher confidence
  - Reduces surprise forecast misses that damage credibility
  - Enables better hiring/investment planning
  - **Value: $50K-100K annually** (estimated value of forecast credibility to board/investors)

- **Sales Compensation Accuracy**: Accurate forecast enables accurate commission forecasting
  - Reduces quarter-end scrambles to hit budget
  - Sales team more motivated by accurate targets
  - **Value: $30K-50K annually** (reduced demotivation/churn from forecast miss surprises)

- **Revenue Planning**: Accurate forecast enables better capacity planning
  - Marketing can allocate budget to support correct pipeline size
  - CS team can plan headcount for expected revenue
  - **Value: $50K-100K annually** (better resource allocation ROI)

**Subtotal - Forecast Accuracy: $130K-250K Annual Value**
