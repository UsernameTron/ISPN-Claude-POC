# Sorensen Skills Mapping: Claude Capabilities for VP Sales/Marketing/CS

**Executive Summary:** Sorensen (VP Sales/Marketing/CS) requires multi-disciplinary Claude Skills addressing presentation excellence, marketing content velocity, customer intelligence, and sales operations. This mapping prioritizes high-impact capabilities that directly influence revenue acceleration and customer retention.

---

## Skill 1: Presentation & Proposal Generation

### Relevance
Sorensen leads sales presentations, investor updates, and customer executive meetings. Claude's presentation skills dramatically reduce preparation time while ensuring message consistency across all customer-facing materials.

### Specific Use Cases
- **Sales Deck Generation**: Create comprehensive pitch decks customized by prospect industry/size in <15 minutes vs. 4-6 hours manual
- **Quarterly Business Reviews (QBRs)**: Auto-generate customer health dashboards with performance metrics, recommendations, and renewal probability assessments
- **Investor/Board Updates**: Package quarterly revenue metrics, pipeline health, and strategic initiatives into executive presentations
- **Proposal Customization**: Rapid iteration on proposals for different buyer personas/company sizes with personalized value propositions

### Quantified Value
- **Time Savings**: 70% reduction in presentation creation time (4-6 hours → 1-1.5 hours for complex decks)
- **Consistency**: Eliminates brand/messaging inconsistencies across 50+ sales materials
- **Revenue Impact**: Sales team spends 2+ additional hours/week selling vs. deck-building = **$150K-200K in incremental annual pipeline** (assuming $200/hour average deal value creation)
- **Close Rate Improvement**: Better-structured, customer-specific proposals increase win rates by estimated 8-12% on high-touch deals

### MCP Integration
**Canva MCP** (Primary Integration)
- Claude generates presentation briefs → Canva MCP auto-creates visual deck designs
- Maintains brand consistency through templated color palettes and design systems
- Enables non-design team members to generate professional-grade materials
- Real-time iteration: feedback → Claude re-outlines → Canva re-renders (5-minute cycles)

**Optional: Google Workspace Integration**
- Export presentations directly to Google Slides for collaborative team editing
- Version control and stakeholder feedback loops

## Skill 3: Customer Intelligence & Health Scoring

### Relevance
Sorensen needs real-time visibility into customer health for both sales (expansion opportunities) and CS (retention risk). Claude transforms raw customer data into actionable intelligence, enabling proactive interventions.

### Specific Use Cases
- **Customer Health Dashboards**: Synthesize product usage data, support ticket frequency/sentiment, CSM notes into numerical health scores
- **Expansion Opportunity Identification**: Analyze customer usage patterns, support interactions for upsell/cross-sell signals
- **Churn Risk Detection**: Identify at-risk accounts based on usage decline, ticket sentiment changes, renewal timeline
- **Customer Segment Analysis**: Group customers by growth trajectory, product adoption, support intensity; tailor engagement model
- **Renewal Probability Forecasting**: Predict renewal likelihood based on product adoption, implementation success, ROI realization

### Quantified Value
- **Proactive Interventions**: Early identification of at-risk accounts enables 1-2 month earlier intervention windows
- **Churn Reduction**: Assuming 12% annual churn rate and $200K ACV, 3% churn reduction = **$600K annual revenue saved**
- **Expansion Revenue**: Systematic identification of expansion-ready customers increases expansion revenue by 20-30% = **$200K-400K annually** (assumes $1M existing customer base)
- **CS Resource Efficiency**: Automated health scoring eliminates 5-10 hours/week of manual analysis; enables 1 CSM to cover 40+ accounts vs. 25-30

### MCP Integration
**HubSpot MCP** (Primary Integration)
- Claude pulls customer data (product usage, ticket history, NPS scores, renewal dates)
- Generates health scores, risk assessments, expansion recommendations
- Pushes health scores and recommended actions back to HubSpot for CSM action
- Feeds expansion/churn alerts to sales team via automated workflows

**Optional: Slack MCP** (Alerting)
- Critical alerts (high churn risk, major expansion opportunity) → #customer-success or assigned CSM via Slack
- Weekly health summaries for customer leadership team

## Skill 4: Sales Pipeline & Forecast Analysis

### Relevance
Sorensen owns board-level revenue forecasting and pipeline health accountability. Claude transforms deal data into predictive intelligence, improving forecast accuracy by 15-25% while surfacing coaching opportunities.

### Specific Use Cases
- **Forecast Accuracy Improvement**: Analyze deal stage timing, win/loss patterns, salesperson consistency; model probability-weighted pipeline
- **Deal Coaching Recommendations**: Identify deals at risk of stalling; recommend next-best actions based on historical win patterns
- **Sales Activity Analysis**: Correlate activity levels (calls, meetings, proposals) with deal progression and close probability
- **Salesperson Performance Segmentation**: Identify top performers' selling patterns; codify best practices
- **Pipeline Health Reporting**: Monthly/quarterly pipeline composition, velocity analysis, coverage ratios

### Quantified Value
- **Forecast Accuracy**: Improvement from ±15-20% variance to ±5-10% variance
  - Enables better resource planning, board confidence, reduced surprise misses
  - **Estimated value: $50K-100K** in reduced forecast shock and better resource allocation
- **Deal Recovery**: Early identification of stalling deals enables 8-12% recovery of otherwise-lost deals
  - Assuming $2M monthly pipeline and 8% recovery = **$160K additional quarterly revenue**
- **Salesperson Productivity**: Best-practice sharing across sales team increases conversion rates 5-10%
  - Assuming $5M annual pipeline and 7% improvement = **$350K annual incremental revenue**
- **Sales Cycle Acceleration**: Coaching recommendations reduce average sales cycle by 1-2 weeks
  - Enables 1-2 additional deals/quarter per salesperson = **$250K-500K per quarter additional revenue potential**

### MCP Integration
**HubSpot MCP** (Primary Integration)
- Claude pulls real-time pipeline data (deal stage, close date, deal size, stage duration)
- Generates forecast reports with probability adjustments, risk flags
- Provides deal-level coaching recommendations (next best actions, risk factors)
- Pushes forecast and coaching alerts to sales managers and Sorensen

**Optional: Google Workspace Integration**
- Export forecast reports to Google Sheets for board presentation preparation
- Real-time pipeline dashboards updated daily
