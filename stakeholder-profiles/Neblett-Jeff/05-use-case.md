## What Makes This Possible (Executive Language)

### Model Context Protocol (MCP)
**Translation**: "Your systems talking directly to AI"
**What It Means**: Claude Desktop connects securely to Asana, Slack, and your files - everywhere your data lives. No more copy-paste, no more "let me check that system." Ask a question, get an answer that considers everything.

### Claude Skills
**Translation**: "Pre-built expertise for exactly what you need"
**What It Means**: Instead of teaching AI your processes each time, Skills understand ISPN's specific workflows, financial models, and strategic frameworks. It's like having your best analyst's knowledge built in.
