## Q&A Handling (5 minutes)

### Anticipated Questions & Responses

**Q: "Is my data secure?"**
**A**: "Absolutely. Watch this..." [Show security settings]
"Your data never leaves ISPN's control. Claude connects to your systems using your existing credentials. It's like having an analyst with your exact permissions - they can only see what you can see. Every query is logged for audit trails. This meets PE-grade security requirements."

**Q: "What if it makes a mistake?"**
**A**: "Great question. Let me show you something in the Excel model..." [Open the model]
"See these formulas? You can verify every calculation. This isn't black-box AI - it's transparent analysis you can validate. Plus, you're the CEO - you review before distributing, just like you do with your CFO's work today."

**Q: "How much does this cost?"**
**A**: "The software is $200 per month. But let's be honest about the real cost discussion. Your time is worth roughly $500 per hour to ISPN. You're saving 70 hours monthly. That's $35,000 in monthly value. The ROI is 175:1. The question isn't cost - it's how fast we can deploy this."

**Q: "Can my team use this?"**
**A**: "Absolutely, and they should. Imagine Scott having this for financial analysis, Charlie for operations, Ty for sales forecasting. But here's my recommendation: You use it exclusively for two weeks first. Get comfortable, see the impact, then we roll out to your directs. Within 30 days, your entire leadership team operates at this level."

**Q: "What if I'm not technical?"**
**A**: "Jeff, you just used it yourself. Did you write any code? Configure any APIs? You had a conversation. That's the breakthrough - it works the way you think, not the way computers work."

**Q: "How long to implement?"**
**A**: "You're looking at it implemented. This is your live system. We can do a 30-minute personalization session tomorrow to tune it to your preferences, but you could use this for tomorrow's board call if needed."

**Recovery**: "This is exactly what you'd see with a stable connection. Let's get IT to verify your setup after this."
