# Scott Lauber - President & CFO Profile

Scott Lauber serves as President & Chief Financial Officer of ISPN Network Services, iGLASS Networks, and 20 South Partners. A co-founder of 20 South Partners alongside Jeff Neblett, he has been instrumental in ISPN's financial strategy and operational growth since acquiring the company in 2020.
